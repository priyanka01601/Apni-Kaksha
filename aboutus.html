About Us

Apni Kaksha is a learning platform for the students to find tutor outside the class based on skills/subject they need more attention. The system is designed to provide a way to make a class outside class. We believe that in an age of cutthroat academic competition, no student should face any difficulty in learning due to lack of guidance. To combat this issue, we created this sleek portal to help connect students to the right tutors. 

We believe that with technology we can transcend traditional boundaries and truly enable customized self -paced learning by helping students learn more in less time and at a lower cost. All our tutors are knowledgeable and patient, they are also mentors who provide the guidance you need to succeed. We give personal attention to each student. We’re not just here to be your educators, but to also help you be more confident and independent.
